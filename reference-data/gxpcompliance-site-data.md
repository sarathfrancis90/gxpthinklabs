## Page 5: CSV (Computer System Validation)

### Hero
- **Title**: Computer System Validation (CSV)

### Services Section
- **Intro**: Validating and maintaining GxP-regulated IT systems requires a risk-based approach that aligns with industry best practices, GAMP guidelines, and regulatory requirements such as 21 CFR Part 11 and Annex 11. Whether you are implementing a new system, assessing an existing one, or transitioning to CSA principles, we provide the expertise and documentation needed to ensure compliance.

**Service Items:**
- CSV/CSA Strategy Development – Defining the appropriate validation and assurance approach based on system risk and regulatory expectations.
- Validation Planning & Execution – Developing and implementing:
  - Validation Plans, User & Functional Requirements (URS, FRS, DS), Risk-Based Testing (IQ/OQ/PQ), and Traceability Matrices.
  - Cloud and SaaS System Validation, including QMS, LIMS, ERP, MES, and Document Management platforms.
  - Qualification of custom software (i.e., GAMP Category 5).
  - Qualification of Data Analytics platforms (i.e., Power BI reporting).
  - Development of SharePoint Qualification and Security guidelines.
- Data Integrity & Part 11/Annex 11 Compliance Reviews – Assessing electronic records, signatures, and audit trails to identify compliance gaps.
- Remediation & Continuous Compliance Support – Updating existing validation programs to reflect current regulatory expectations and industry trends.

## Key Industry Terms & Compliance Framework References

- **GxP**: Good Practice regulations (GMP, GLP, GCP, etc.)
- **GAMP**: Good Automated Manufacturing Practice (guidelines for validation)
- **21 CFR Part 11**: FDA regulation on electronic records and electronic signatures
- **Annex 11**: EU GMP regulation on computerized systems
- **CSV**: Computer System Validation
- **CSA**: Computer Software Assurance
- **SDLC**: System Development Life Cycle
- **IQ/OQ/PQ**: Installation Qualification / Operational Qualification / Performance Qualification
- **URS**: User Requirements Specification
- **FRS**: Functional Requirements Specification
- **DS**: Design Specification
- **QMS**: Quality Management System
- **LIMS**: Laboratory Information Management System
- **ERP**: Enterprise Resource Planning
- **MES**: Manufacturing Execution System
- **MDF/IDF**: Main Distribution Frame / Intermediate Distribution Frame
